Our Story

Born from a passion
for perfection

Coffee Barly was born from a simple obsession: the perfect cup. Founded in 2019, we believe that great coffee is not just a drink — it is a ritual, a pause, a moment of pure presence in a rushing world.

Every bean we source tells a story of its land. Every roast we craft is a conversation between heat and time. Every cup we serve is our promise of quality, warmth, and intention.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you offer dairy-free milk alternatives?
Yes! We offer oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, and coconut milk as substitutes at no extra charge. Our baristas are trained to optimize texturing for each milk type.
Can I purchase whole beans or ground coffee to take home?
Absolutely. We sell our small-batch roasted beans in 250g and 500g bags. Just ask at the counter or browse our retail shelf near the entrance. We also offer custom grinding to your preferred brew method.
How do I book the private event space?
Use our reservation form below or email us directly at contactglanders@gmail.com. We accommodate private bookings for up to 40 guests. Please allow at least 72 hours notice for weekday events and 1 week for weekends.
Are your memberships auto-renewing?
Yes, all memberships renew monthly. You can cancel, pause, or upgrade at any time with no penalty — simply contact us at least 5 days before your renewal date.
Do you cater for corporate offices or events?
Yes, we offer bespoke catering packages for corporate events, photo shoots, private parties, and office deliveries. Get in touch via email and we will create a custom proposal for you.
